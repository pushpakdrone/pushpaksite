About US

Pushpak Drone is a product and service focused UAV Technology organization powered by Artificial Intelligence and Computer Vision, that develops drones for a variety of mapping and agriculture applications.

Pushpak Drone is proud of its unrivaled ability to push the boundaries of what is possible with unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs) and encourage the use of this technology to improve productivity and security.

Our Drones boast high performance, dependability, and the freedom to complete the task with minimal stress.
